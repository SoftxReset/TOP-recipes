Pad See Ew

Description

Pad See Ew is the saltier stir fried noodles to its sweet and nutty cousin, Pad Thai. Its name meaning "stir fried soy sauce noodles," Pad See Ew is a popular Thai street food.

Tip to making Pad See Ew

The two pivotal components in making Pad See Ew are the sauce and the caramlizing of the noodles. It's common to see recipes instruct the use of just soy sauce and sugar, but that's the easy way of doing it! But that could get in the way of getting the right level of caramlization on the noodles.

The solution? Separate the noodles from the stir fry, making it easier to caramelize.

Ingredients

Sauce:

  • Dark soy sauce
  • Ordinary or light soy sauce
  • Oyster sauce
  • Vinegar
  • Sugar

Stir Fry:

  • Wide rice noodles
  • Broccoli
  • Strips of chicken
  • Egg
  • Garlic

Making Pad See Ew

  1. Cook the chicken and vegetables first, then set off to the side within the wok.
  2. Crack the egg, scramble immediately.
  3. Remove the stir fry and place onto a plate.
  4. Add the sauce and noodles into the wok.
  5. Toss the noodles quickly as the sauce disperses throughout the noodles and the caramlization starts.
  6. After the noodles have caramlized, add the stir fry back into the wok.
